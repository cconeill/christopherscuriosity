AD Users and Computers slow; kill NETBIOS

Recently when I’ve been working in Active Directory Users and Computers in my admin VM I’ve noticed that my first interaction would take 5 or 10 seconds to register. A quick bit of googling suggested that disabling NETBIOS over TCP/IP would provide a quick workaround. Lo-and-behold this fixed it right up.

To disable NETBIOS over TCP/IP just go to your NIC’s IPv4 properties, then Advanced, then, WINS, then disabled NETBIOS over TCP/IP.
